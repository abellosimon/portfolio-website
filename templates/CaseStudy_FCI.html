Case Study: FCI❤️

2018-2020

Fundacion Cardio Infantil (FCI) is a leading cardiovascular care provider. As healthcare evolved, FCI recognized the need to enhance its digital presence to better serve patients and boost donations.

With rising operational costs and the increasing demand for advanced medical equipment and services, FCI was seeking a partner that would help them create a digital presence aligned with their growth objectives without compromising their humanitarian mission and patient care.

As the Account Executive (and later Brand Planner) for the healthcare client, my objective was to reflect the changing needs of FCI and manage their transition into the digital space:

  • I spearheaded the launch of FCI's revamped website, ensuring it was both user-friendly and representative of their brand ethos.
  • Oversaw a team encompassing social media content creators, graphic designers, and strategists, ensuring consistent messaging and branding across all digital platforms.

Results

  • Successfully renegotiated the contract, achieving a 20% increase, which translated to a lucrative $220k ARR opportunity.
  • Deepened our ties with FCI, evolving from a mere service provider to a valued strategic ally.
  • Clinched a Silver Effie award for our blood donation campaigns.
