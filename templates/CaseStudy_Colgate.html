2020

In 2020, the world experienced an unexpected shift due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Businesses were forced to rethink their strategies and quickly adapt to the changing landscape from physical to digital.

My client at Colgate was amid preparing for the launch of our new Bamboo toothbrush, a product aimed at environmentally conscious consumers. Originally, the plan was to host physical customer events to introduce the product. However, the pandemic-imposed restrictions on such gatherings.

How to effectively promote the environmentally-friendly features of the Bamboo toothbrush in a purely digital realm, especially when the original strategy was grounded in physical engagement?

We recognized the power of influencers in shaping consumer perceptions, especially in the sustainable and travel sectors. With this in mind, we partnered with four prominent sustainable-friendly and travel influencers for a unique digital campaign.

The campaign was designed as a virtual tree planting contest. Each influencer was tasked with sharing a post about our Bamboo toothbrush. The engagement metric was unique: the audience was encouraged to react using the tree emoji. The influencer garnering the most tree emojis would win the largest forest planted in their hometown. All influencers would have forests planted on their behalf, but the sizes would decrease based on the number of reactions.

Results

  • Sales: The Bamboo toothbrush experienced a successful launch with a significant boost in sales.
  • Share of Voice: There was a 9% increase in our share of voice.
  • Engagement: Overall, Colgate Colombia experienced a 7% increase in engagement across all digital channels.
